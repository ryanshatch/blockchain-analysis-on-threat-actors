            <h2>DPRK / TraderTraitor / Lazarus:</h2> The FBI officially attributed the February 2025 Bybit theft to DPRK-linked “TraderTraitor” actors and published Ethereum addresses that either held, or previously held, stolen Bybit assets.
            <br>TRM also supported the attribution and traced the movement of those funds, noting that a large portion of the stolen value was moved across chains, including into BTC.
            <br>With that being said, the FBI had already published a separate set of BTC addresses tied to earlier DPRK TraderTraitor activity, after affiliated actors moved roughly 1,580 BTC from prior heists.
            <br>Those BTC addresses are older than the Bybit incident itself, but they still matter. They provide high-confidence, official BTC pivots connected to the same broader DPRK TraderTraitor / Lazarus activity cluster.
